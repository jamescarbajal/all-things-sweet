ORDER

Ordering is as easy as 1, 2, 3:

  1. Message us on Facebook Messenger with as much info as possible, including one or more Inspo Pics!
  2. We'll hammer out the details together and give you a quote/price before finalizing.
  3. Make your 50% non-refundable deposit to reserve your order and pick-up date!

    Payment methods to be discussed upon finalizing order.

In order to expedite the ordering process, we ask that your initial message to us contains as much of your order details as possible, including types of desserts, flavors, sizes, themes, pickup date, and an Inspo Pic.

We currently manage all of our orders through the Facebook Messenger App. You can also message us from this webpage using the “Ask us” Messenger icon at the bottom right, or you can find us on our Facebook Page.

For all orders, including catering and wedding cakes, please follow the instructions above.

If you're still a little confused, why not check out our FAQ to clear things up?

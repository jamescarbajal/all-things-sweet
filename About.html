ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

All Things Sweet is owned and operated by a single person. We're a home-based dessert shop selling from Fresno, California, in the beautiful Central Valley. We offer homemade cakes, pastries, and other desserts, most of which are made from scratch using proprietary recipes and ingredients. We use only fresh fruits, only home-made icing, we print all of our own toppers, mold our own fondant and have an extensive collection of flowers and other decorations. Please message us 24/7 on Facebook Messenger, because we're always excited to make your cake.

OUR HISTORY

Millie started All Things Sweet in 2018 after leaving her retail job of over 10 years. She'd always wanted to own her own business and always loved to bake, so she finally decided to take the leap. While she admits it can be a challenge, there hasn't been a single moment that she's regretted following her dream. Now, she can offer her own version of gourmet cakes and desserts to the world. Her goal is to have a store-front in the next few years, so keep an eye out!

OUR VALUES

A sense of community and purpose is ingrained within the very fabric of All Things Sweet; we believe in giving back to the community. From making sure to represent our Hmong heritage, to giving to the less fortunate through Cake4Kids, to sharing techniques and tips on the Cake Decorating Tutorials Facebook page, we hope to make big impacts through small gestures.

SUPPORT YOUR LOCAL BUSINESS!!

Thank you for supporting your local bakery! When you do so, you don't just help a business and the few good people behind it, you help to bolster your local economy. By choosing small businesses, you also discover products, services, cultures, ideas and people you didn't know could be right in your back yard. Thank you for choosing All Things Sweet!

FAQ

It's not so bad! Just follow these 3 easy steps:

  1. Message us on Facebook Messenger with as much info as possible, including types of desserts, flavors, sizes, themes, pickup date, and one or more Inspo Pics (see next question). It's ok if your initial message is extra wordy... we prefer it! To make things easier, feel free to familiarize yourself with our Menu and our Gallery.
  2. We'll message you back in order to hammer out the details and give you a quote/price before finalizing/reserving. We want to get your order just right.
  3. Make your 50% non-refundable deposit to reserve your order and pick-up date!

    Orders are not reserved before deposit. Payment methods to be discussed upon finalizing order.

We currently manage our orders almost exclusively through Facebook Messenger, where you will discuss the details of your order with the Owner/Operator/Baker herself!! You can also message using the “Ask us” chat plug-in at the bottom right of this page or find us on Facebook.

An inspiration image (we call it an “Inspo Pic”) is an image showing a cake or other dessert you might like to imitate. This might be an image you find online or a cake we've previously made (Hint: Take a peek at our Gallery and Facebook Page!).

Feel free to send one or more Inspo Pics when initially messaging us for your order or quote. Your Inspo Pic(s) should demonstrate dessert features you'd like your cake to include regarding theme, size, color, decorations, or any combination of these or other features.

Please keep in mind, though, that any designs and flavors that we consider non-standard will likely add to the cost of your order. It should also be noted that we do not cover the entirety of our cakes in fondant nor do we write on our cakes. Sorry!
